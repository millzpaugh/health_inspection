Bakery ♥ : The Health Inspector Cracks Down

Do you love muffins and clean kitchens?

I do! So I decided to map the health inspection ratings from bakeries around DC.

*All ratings were retrieved from the DC Health Regulation & Licensing Administration. The risk scale is 1 (lowest risk) - 5 (highest risk). 0 indicates no rating.

  • Rating 1 - 2: Great spot!
  • Rating 3: Start moving the other direction.
  • Rating 4 - 5: Disaster might ensue.

    • If you want to know about delis, restaurants, fish markets and more, check out the data!
